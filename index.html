Transparency · open methodology
How FDRS is calculated
Every country gets one Food Dependency & Resilience Score from 0–100. It's a weighted blend of five components, all derived from public data. Every weight is documented. Every source is cited.
Live Feed
Live Disturbances
Real-time food security disruptions — droughts, conflicts, price shocks and humanitarian crises. Live data from GDACS and ReliefWeb; baseline events curated from FAO/WFP/IPC/FEWS NET reports (no live FEWS feed in pipeline).
About FoodShield AI
Food security is an underpublicised systemic risk.
FoodShield AI exists to make global food-system fragility legible — to anyone, not just specialists. One score, 177 countries + 50 US states, 17 public data feeds refreshed daily, no paywall. Every metric is labeled sourced, manual, or modeled so readers can audit the foundation under each number.
